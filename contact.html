Why Explore Spokane?

Spokane is one of the most underrated outdoor cities in the Pacific Northwest. With forests, rivers, parks, and miles of trails woven directly through the city, it offers endless opportunities for exploration.

For runners, Spokane is especially unique. Within minutes you can run along the Spokane River, explore forested trails, or loop through historic parks like Manito Park. Whether you're training for a race or simply getting outside, Spokane makes every run feel like an adventure.
